Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Crispy edges, chewy middle. You don't want to miss out on making these cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cups chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F

  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the
    eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to
    batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls
    onto ungreased pans.

  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
