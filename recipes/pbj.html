Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A staple of American childhood foods, peanut butter and jelly sandwich, or PB&J for short, is a sandwich consisting of two slices of bread, one with a layer of peanut butter, and the other one with jelly or jam.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Strawberry or Grape)
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Lay your 2 slices of bread down
  2. Spread peanut butter on top of one of the slices of bread
  3. Speard jelly on top of the other slice of bread
  4. Take one of the slices and place it down on the other slice top side down
  5. Enjoy!
